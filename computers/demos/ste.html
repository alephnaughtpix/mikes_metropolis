To run these demos, you need an Atari STE. Some of these demos may work on Falcon 030s as well. Most of these demos will work on a half megabyte system, but 1 meg is usually preferred. You'll see what I mean if you have a half meg system, and you have to keep reloading the selection menus!!

Brain Damage:

This is one of the most impressive STE-only demos ever released! It borrows heavily from the design sense of Amiga demos, but also adds the brute processor power effects of Atari demos. (The opening sequence is something else.)

By: Aggression, Kruz (19 Aug 1996)
