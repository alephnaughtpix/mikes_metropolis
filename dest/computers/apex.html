Apex Media

For a short time, you'll be able to download a demo of one of the best graphics programs for the Falcon 030, Apex Media. This program is packed with features such as:

  • Full FLI/FLX animation support.
  • Comprehensive drawing and brush facilites.
  • Impressive filtering and masking options.
  • Aliasing and "mip-mapping" techniques to soften pixellated pictures.
  • Fast morphing/distortion using the DSP.
  • Support of most picture formats, including the soon-to-be dead GIF!
  • ... And probably loads of other features I haven't seen yet.
