HOW TO JOIN THE SNP

[ Home | Info | Events | Links | Contacts | Join! ]
There are many ways you can join and take part in the Scottish National Party...

If you are...

  • ...a student at Glasgow University...
... You can join Glasgow University SNP Students! Membership is only 2 ukp per year. To find out more, email me, or contact a member of the Executive.

  • ...a student from outside Glasgow University...
... You can join SNP Students. This is the official student wing of the SNP, and is composed of various nationalist clubs in various higher education establishments around Scotland. Membership is open to anyone in full-time higher education. If you're reading this in a university where there is no SNP Students branch, then you can join as a headquarters member. To find out if there is an SNP Students branch in your area, you can go the branch directory of the official SNP Students WWW pages here.
  • ...under 26 years of age...
... You can join the Young Scottish Nationalists (YSN). Also known as Young Scots for Independence, this is the official youth wing of the SNP, and membership is open to everyone under the age of 26. If you wish to find out about the nearest branch, go to their WWW pages, or contact them through the SNP's WWW Pages. As with SNP Students, if there are no local branches, you can become an HQ member.
  • ...all or none of the above...
... You can join the Scottish National Party. You join either join your local SNP branch, or join as a headquarters member. To find out more information, go to the official SNP pages.

Note that being a member of one group does not necessarily preclude being a member of another. For example, if you're under 26 and a student at Glasgow University, as well as being a member of GU SNP Students (and therefore a member of SNP Students), you could also be a member of the YSN, and a member of your local SNP branch.
