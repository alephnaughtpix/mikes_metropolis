What's in these pages?

There are three main strands here. (And a fourth to come very soon!)

The first is the links page. Rather obviously this contains links to other WWW servers! If there's any links I hear of, and I haven't yet put them in the other pages (See Below), then they'll go in here first.

The second is home computer scenes! In it you'll be able to download a whole load of demos and disk magazines, and read various pages on Atari and Amiga stuff. There's also links to other interesting Atari and Amiga sites.

The third is decent music! Don't worry, I'll explain what I mean when I say "decent music", so you'll know whether to avoid this bit or not, but given that my musical tastes are pretty widely varied, the odds are that there will be at least something you might like in here.
