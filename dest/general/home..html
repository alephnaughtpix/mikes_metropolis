Michael James' Home Page / FAQ Thingy


Who is this ghostly figure?

It's a passport photograph of the maintainer of this vast sprawling mini-empire of WWW pages, and his name is Michael James.

What does this Michael James person do?

He's a Science student at Glasgow University . He's in his final year and is currently studying Mathematics .

Why's he loose on the World Wide Web?

He got an account with the Computing Service and has been on the Internet ever since. Recently, he's found out how to set up his own publically accessible WWW pages, and here they are!

Does he do anything else in the University?

Oh yes, he's involved with a couple of non-academic societies:
  • He's the treasurer of the GU WWW Society.
  • He's an Ordinary Science member of GU Students Representative Council.
  • He's in charge of the WWW Server for GU SNP Students.
  • He's also in the Science Fiction Society as well- it's not all serious!

Anything else that he does?

Funny you should say that, because he is quite involved in the home computer scene , particularly on the Atari scene, where is involved with the demo/coding crew Digitech. He's also been involved on the Amiga, as a musician for the group Adrenalin, and has done the in-game music for the Archimedes game Hi-Risc Racing. And talking of music , he has a fair amount interest in that side of things as well. He even uses his Atari for making music with MIDI!
