What the massive music section is all about....

Well, first things first. It's massive because I haven't split it up yet!

Anyway, you might have looked at the bands/artists list and wondered how I could make such a wierd and varied choice. Well, put it this way, as far as styles go, as long as it's does not smell funny. ;)

That pretty neatly summarises my choice of tastes, but you might detect a slight bias in my the newer names of my list towards techno/ambient/synthy stuff, and not so much of the indie/rock band names you might associate with students. Well, in my opinion of a lot of indie/rock today isn't quite as fresh as the techno/ambient/synthy stuff around today. (Which is about as mildly as I can put it without offending a lot of people.) Not that there aren't exceptions- Oasis, Pulp, and Blur are good examples of indie bands that matter, though the way that the weekly music papers treat them like "gods" does tend to emphasise how great an exception they are to the general rule.

Of course exactly the same fate is going to befall a lot of the techno/ambient/synthy stuff of today, that's progress for you... If you want to read my views about certain luddite tendancies in "indie" circles, then go here.
