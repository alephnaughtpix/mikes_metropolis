Local SNP Branches in and around Glasgow University

There are local branches of the SNP all over Scotland, and even outside Scotland. Here are the nearest local branches to Glasgow University.

General Election Update: Sandra White adopted as candidate for Kelvin Constituency.

In a packed meeting at Partick Burgh halls on Thu 25th April, Sandra White was adopted as the prospective Parliamentary candidate for Kelvin constituency. Speakers at the meeting included our Rectorial candidate of last year, Dorothy Grace Elder and Roseanna Cunningham MP.
