This part of my links section deals with dorky links!! This is a bit like the Dorky pictures section of my Decent Music section, except it's general links. Occasionally I've come across links that are so extremely terrible, they are worthy of a mention in Mirsky's Worst of the Web. So I'd mail the details off to Mirsky. They weren't followed through that much because:

  • Mirsky would often be so busy, he often couldn't include every link people sent him.
  • Because he was so busy, Mirsky has now finished Worst of the Web (1st Nov 96).
  • Some of my links might not make much sense to people from the US. .

So any link I contribute to Worst of the Web, that Mirsky can't include goes in this jolly little section!

Let the Dorky Links commence!
