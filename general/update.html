IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT! (14/6/96)

Some of you who have been reading this page will know that it's been gathering a bit of dust for the past few months. Why? Well, as my pages have been getting more and more complex and I've had more to do in the outside (real) world, I've had less time to actually update the update page! The problem is that I've often spent a lot of time radically changing parts of the Metropolis, and I hardly have any time left to do all this "Last updated..." business up the directory tree!

Yes, I know it's pretty stupid, but you try doing it yourself with little time! Anyway, enough of my whingeing, because !am currently moving towards remedying this situation with lovely little Perl scripts to automate a large part of this updating nonsense. Already, I've developed a script to trawl through the directories and compile a contents page and alphabetical index. Even better, I can get the date they were created and last altered, so hopefully soon, this page will live again as an automatically generated update page, and you won't have to see too much of these rambling explanations......
